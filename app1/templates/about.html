Our Agendas

• Replace individual events by a series of well-structured events to aim for a greater outcome.
• Bridge the gap between ideas and skills.
• Provide exposure to modern frameworks and technologies (which are not covered in the curriculum.)
• Increase feasibility even to complete large projects by breaking them down into a series of achievable tasks.
• Provide a flexible and dynamic relationship between students and projects.
• Follow a product-oriented approach.
• Promote peer-to-peer learning among students to enhance the growth of skills.
• Provide the students with an environment closer to workplace associations and expectations thus helping them build a better candidature for companies.
• Create an environment where-in both the problem statements, as well as the solutions, are provided by the students.
• Orientation towards learning and implementing multiple technologies.
• Provide acknowledgment to the participants/contributors on successful completion of the goal.
• A platform to put forth ideas with a potential to be converted into meaningful products and projects.
